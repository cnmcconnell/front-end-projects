What is the weather like?

The weather is typically sunny and the temperate averages 61° F yearly.

Is there parking available downtown?

There are more than 800 free street parking spots as well as 14 parking facilities within walking distance. Simply follow the green "P"

How can I ride the Trolley?

The trolley does not have a fare and is operational Thursdays and Fridays from 6-11 p.m., Saturdays from 10 a.m. to 11 p.m. and Sundays from 1-8 p.m.

What are the bike lockers?

Bike lockers offer renters a safe way to secure their bikes. Rental terms are $50 for six months.

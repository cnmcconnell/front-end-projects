I would like to make a large order, how long will it take to assemble the computers?

Typically, each system has a total assembly, testing, and release duration of about one day. Each one of our assemblers can work on about 8 systems at a time. This is particularly true during our quality assurance phase of the assembly process, where the systems go through automated tests to validate the integrity of each system.

What tests do you perform during the Quality Assurance phase of assembly?

We typically perform a long-term burn-in test, to assure that each component can operate at maximum capacity for extended period of time. We also perform baseline benchmarks on all components to assure that they meet, or exceed the median performance index of identical components.
